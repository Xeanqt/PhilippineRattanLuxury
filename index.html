Why Choose Rattan?

Rattan is a fast-growing, vine-like palm found mainly in Indonesia, with the Philippines also being a notable source. Known for its strength, flexibility, and durability, rattan is widely used in crafting furniture, baskets, and tools, including weapons for Filipino martial arts. While traditionally passed down through generations, rattan crafting also serves as a livelihood for many, showcasing unique styles and techniques across cultures. Best suited for indoor use, it remains a symbol of cultural heritage and utility.

About us

We are Grade 12 ABM students from Fidelis FAITH Colleges:

First Asia Institute of Technology and Humanities. This website

is our major project for the second quarter of the school year,

focused on showcasing rattan products and their production

processes. Our goal is to provide valuable insights into rattans

uses, history, and craftsmanship. Through this project, we aim

to raise awareness about this sustainable material and its

significance in both traditional and modern design.
